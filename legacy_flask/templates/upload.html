CSV Format Requirements

Your CSV file should contain the following columns:

  • Patient Age - Age of the patient
  • Patient Gender - Male, Female, or Other
  • Suspected Drug - Name of the drug (required)
  • Reaction - Adverse reaction or side effect (required)
  • Severity - Mild, Moderate, Severe, or Life-Threatening
  • Causality - WHO-UMC assessment
  • Seriousness - Serious or Non-Serious
  • Outcome - Treatment outcome
  • Follow-up - Follow-up status
  • Reporter Name - Name of reporting healthcare professional
  • Notes - Additional clinical notes

Example CSV Header:

Patient Age,Patient Gender,Suspected Drug,Reaction,Severity,Causality,Seriousness,Outcome,Follow-up,Reporter Name,Notes
